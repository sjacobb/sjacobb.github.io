about me

I’m the kind of person that loves new experiences. I have cold feet before, sure, but ultimately I still do it. That’s what matters, right? Growing up in Wilson, North Carolina, new experiences were just about as easy to come by as a McDonald’s with a working ice cream machine. So when I got to Australia, the world suddenly got a whole lot bigger.

Describing one’s study abroad experience as the gateway into discovering one’s life purpose is cliche, so I won’t do it. Instead I’ll describe it as life changing.

Coming back my junior year to the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (Go Heels) after a semester in Australia, I realized that what I want to do with my life is create. Now I’m by no means an artist, and I’ll never try to be. But what I can do is create through advertising.

This doesn’t always mean copywriting. I’m open to creating strategy. Or creating digital advertising. Heck, I’ll even create a spreadsheet if I need to. My point is I love creating and I love advertising. Anything dealing with both will make me (almost) as happy as my time in Australia.
